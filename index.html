Marlboro High School

Trunk or Treat

Come join us at Marlboro High School's Trunk or Treat event! The event takes place in the parking lot at Marlboro High School.

Join us for a fun-filled, nut-free event! This free event is open to children ages 12 and under. We'll have great music, exciting games, and face painting (to be confirmed with Lauren Fraser). Donations will be collected for the American Cancer Society. Let's have a blast while making a difference!
